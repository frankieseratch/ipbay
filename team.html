Officers & Partners
Chairman · Co-Founder

Yasuo Matsuo

松尾 康雄 · Long Beach / Tokyo

Founder of Cloverway Inc. (1991–2007) — official representative of Toei Animation for the entire Americas. Trusted intermediary for Shueisha, Kodansha, Shogakukan, and Nippon Animation across three decades.

Architected the institutional relationships that form the foundation of IP Bay's direct publisher access. Personally known to senior contacts at Shueisha, Kodansha, Toei Animation, and Bandai.

At IP Bay, Yasuo chairs publisher strategy and governs the firm's author-first philosophy.

Sakamoto officially introduces IP Bay to the Japanese government — a channel no Hollywood operator has come through before.
