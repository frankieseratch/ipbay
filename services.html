01
IP Licensing & Development

Identifying and securing rights to Japan's most valuable manga and anime properties for Western film, television, and emerging platforms. From initial publisher conversations to signed option agreements.

Shueisha · Kodansha · Kadokawa
02
Hollywood Packaging

Connecting Japanese IP to the producers, studios, and financiers with the creative and commercial capability to get projects made. We package properties for theatrical, streaming, and vertical drama formats.

Film · TV · Vertical Drama
03
Japan Market Access

Providing Western partners with direct publisher relationships and production infrastructure in Japan — including eligibility for Japan's 50% production cash rebate through our Japanese corporate entity.

Japan S-Corp · 50% Cash Rebate Gateway
04
Rights Acquisition & Negotiation

Locating rights holders, structuring option agreements, and negotiating feature, series, and remake rights on behalf of Western producers. We handle the language, the etiquette, and the long-form relationship required to close in Japan.

Options · Remakes · Live-Action Adaptations
05
Co-Production Facilitation

Identifying the right Japanese production partners — studios, animators, writers' rooms — and structuring co-productions that satisfy creator intent, Japanese tax treatment, and Western distribution requirements.

Joint Ventures · Line Production · Local Crew
06
Market Intelligence

On-the-ground research across Japan's publishing, streaming, and theatrical markets. Deal comps, audience data, title performance, and publisher sentiment — delivered by people sitting in Tokyo, not scraping from abroad.

Tokyo Desk · Weekly Publisher Rounds
07
Executive Producing

For projects where alignment with the original author is non-negotiable, IP Bay takes on Executive Producer roles — sitting in the room from development through delivery to ensure the creator's intent survives the production pipeline.

Author-First · Creative Guardianship
08
Talent & Casting Liaison

Direct access to Japan's top-tier talent agencies, voice-over houses, and author representatives — for casting Japanese actors in international productions and coordinating creator appearances, press, and approvals.

Agencies · Voice Talent · Author Approvals
