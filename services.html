Services

What we do.

IP Bay represents Japanese intellectual property to the world. Each engagement is structured around the property and the partners. There is no single deal template. The services below describe the work we take on directly.

Our Mission

For Global Producers

Securing the rights to strong projects.

One of the key challenges facing filmmakers targeting global markets is securing the rights to strong projects. Japan is renowned for the wealth of its intellectual property in comics, animation, and games, each of which has had a significant influence on the craft of global filmmaking.

Our work is to collaborate with thoughtful producers around the world to bring these Japanese stories to the screen, with respect for the creators behind them.

For Japan

With respect for every creator.

We communicate with rights holders throughout the planning and development of each project, with respect for their creative control and their relationship with the work.

We also help find distribution partners in Japan, support the casting of Japanese actors in international productions, and keep rights holders informed about how their work is received in the United States and other territories.
