03 · Services

How we serve
our clients.

Our priority is the quality of our work, not the pursuit of scale. We do business to serve our clients, focusing on the speed and care of our service. We do not make financial investments in productions, and we do not purchase properties on our own.

Our Mission

For Hollywood

Securing the rights to strong projects.

One of the key challenges facing Hollywood filmmakers targeting global markets is securing the rights to strong projects. Japan is renowned for the wealth of its intellectual property in the fields of comics, animation, and games, each of which has had a significant influence on Hollywood filmmaking.

Our mission is to collaborate with thoughtful producers around the world to create filmed entertainment with global appeal, built on these Japanese stories and developed with respect for the creators behind them.

For Japan

With respect for every creator.

We communicate with rights holders throughout the planning and development of each project, with respect for their creative control and their relationship with the work.

We also help find distribution partners in Japan, support the casting of Japanese actors and actresses in international productions, and keep rights holders informed about how their work is received in the United States and other territories.
